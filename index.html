My Story

A Journey Between Cultures, Learning and Exploration

I am Nabin Godar, a curious traveler and lifelong learner from Nepal. My journey has taken me across different cultures, environments, and experiences that have shaped the way I see the world.

I spent several years in Japan, where I studied, experienced a different culture, and learned the value of discipline, independence, and continuous growth.

Through my travels and experiences, I developed a deep curiosity about people, places, ideas, and the stories that connect us all.
